Question 1
Expand: \( (x+4)^2 \)
\( x^2 + 8x + 16 \)

Method 1: FOIL Method

\( (x+4)(x+4) \)
First: \( x \times x = x^2 \)
Outer: \( x \times 4 = 4x \)
Inner: \( 4 \times x = 4x \)
Last: \( 4 \times 4 = 16 \)
Combine: \( x^2 + 4x + 4x + 16 = x^2 + 8x + 16 \)

Method 2: Perfect Square Formula

\( (x+4)^2 = x^2 + 2(x)(4) + 4^2 \)
\( = x^2 + 8x + 16 \)

Question 5
Expand: \( (x-4)^2 \)
\( x^2 - 8x + 16 \)

Method 1: FOIL Method

\( (x-4)(x-4) \)
First: \( x \times x = x^2 \)
Outer: \( x \times (-4) = -4x \)
Inner: \( (-4) \times x = -4x \)
Last: \( (-4) \times (-4) = 16 \)
Combine: \( x^2 - 4x - 4x + 16 = x^2 - 8x + 16 \)

Method 2: Perfect Square Formula

\( (x-4)^2 = x^2 + 2(x)(-4) + (-4)^2 \)
\( = x^2 - 8x + 16 \)
