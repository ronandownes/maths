Factor Perfect Squares

Practice factoring perfect square trinomials

How to Use This Worksheet

  • Look for patterns: \( a^2 + 2ab + b^2 = (a+b)^2 \) or \( a^2 - 2ab + b^2 = (a-b)^2 \)
  • Check if the first and last terms are perfect squares
  • Verify the middle term is twice the product of the square roots
  • Click "Show Answer" to check your solution
  • Click "Show Workings" to see step-by-step methods
  • Use the buttons below to reveal or hide all answers at once
  • Track your progress with the counter
