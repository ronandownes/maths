Question 1
Expand: \( (x+2)^2 \)
\( x^2 + 4x + 4 \)

Method 1: FOIL Method

\( (x+2)(x+2) \)
First: \( x \times x = x^2 \)
Outer: \( x \times 2 = 2x \)
Inner: \( 2 \times x = 2x \)
Last: \( 2 \times 2 = 4 \)
Combine: \( x^2 + 2x + 2x + 4 = x^2 + 4x + 4 \)

Method 2: Split the Brackets

\( (x+2)^2 = (x+2)(x+2) \)
\( = x(x+2) + 2(x+2) \)
\( = x^2 + 2x + 2x + 4 \)
\( = x^2 + 4x + 4 \)
